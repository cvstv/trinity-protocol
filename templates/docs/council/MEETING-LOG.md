# Council Meeting Log

Tracks all council sessions. The Orchestrator appends a row when opening and closing a meeting.

| Meeting | Topic | Date Opened | Date Closed | Status | Decisions | Escalations |
|---------|-------|-------------|-------------|--------|-----------|-------------|

## Status Values

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `open` | Meeting in progress, rounds active |
| `concluded` | All rounds complete, decisions written |
| `human-review` | Contains [MAJOR] decision awaiting human approval |
| `resolved` | All decisions approved and routed to execution |
